International Horticultural Trade Fair

8/25/11 12:00 PM - 8/29/11 7:00 PM

Tull, Austria

International Horticultural Trade Fair in Tulln on the Danube is Austria's largest Flower show and one of the largest european exhibitions on gardening und floristics. It originated from a traditional flowerparade in 1954 and first took place in 1967.
